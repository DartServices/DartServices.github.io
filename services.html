Our Services

DART Outdoor Services provides a full range of landscaping and hardscaping services to commercial and residential customers in Charlotte NC, Fort Mill SC, Columbia SC and surrounding areas. We pride ourselves and specialize in hardscape and irrigation installation. Our team can design, plan, and execute based on the individual wants, needs, and budget of each client.

Landscaping

With extensive experience with plants and trees from the region, we will create and build the perfect landscape for your yard! With hand selected plants and strategic placement, we will make your house stand out in the best way possible.

Hardscape

By adding a patio to your home, you create a whole new outdoor living area that is a great extension to your house. We only use the best materials to ensure you will have a long lasting enjoyable outdoor space. With our high standard of work, we are able to offer a 3 year warranty on our paver patios!

Irrigation

Adding an irrigation system is an effective way to assure your lawn stays looking great and achieve curbside appeal. With one of the best irrigation experts in the industry, we are able to install, troubleshoot, and repair new and existing systems

Fencing

Investing into a good fence can add privacy and appeal to your home. We offer custom fences in various materials including vinyl, aluminum, wood privacy, split rail, etc.

Design & Build

We have our own in-house designer that helps create beautiful 3D designs and drawings to ensure you are 100% satisfied before our team will take that 3D design and turn it into a beautiful completed project.
